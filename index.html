The study was conducted to analyze different sets of demographic data with behavioral risks. Civilians from different occupational industry were asked if Was there a time in the past 12 months when you needed to see a doctor but could not because of cost?". A strong correlation was observed between civilians working in the Sales and Office occupations in general, Retail trade, Arts, Entertainment, Recreation, Accommodation, Food services, Professional, Scientific, Management, Administrative and Waste management services and a positive response as seen in the scatter plot.
